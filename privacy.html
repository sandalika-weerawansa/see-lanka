Privacy Policy

At Traveler, your journey's data is as precious as the experiences themselves. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how we handle your information.

Last Updated: October 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Traveler, operated by SmartTourism Platform. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our websites, software, and services ("Services"). Our goal is to empower your global discovery while keeping your data safe and secure.

By using Traveler, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. We adhere to GDPR, CCPA, and global privacy standards to ensure your rights are respected across all jurisdictions.

2. Data We Collect

We collect and process various types of information to optimize your itinerary, handle bookings, and provide a premium experience.

Account Information

Name, email address, profile picture, and authentication credentials used to personalize your travel dashboard.

Location Data

Precise GPS coordinates (with consent) to provide proximity-based recommendations and itinerary optimization.

Financial Records

Transaction history and budget tracking data. We do not store raw credit card numbers on our local servers.

Technical Logs

IP addresses, browser types, and device identifiers collected automatically to improve service stability and performance.

3. AI Transparency

Traveler uses advanced Machine Learning algorithms to generate personalized itineraries and financial forecasts. Here is how our AI interacts with your data:

01

Human in the Loop

5. Your User Rights

Under global privacy regulations, you have specific rights over your data. You can exercise these at any time via your Profile settings.

01. Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you in a portable format.

02. Erasure

The "Right to be Forgotten" allows you to request the deletion of your account and data.

03. Rectify

Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information in your traveler profile.

6. Security Measures

Industrial-Grade Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures, including AES-256 encryption for data at rest and TLS 1.3 for data in transit. Our infrastructure is protected by multi-factor authentication (MFA) and continuous vulnerability monitoring.
