What is the best way to travel from Colombo to Ella?

The scenic train ride is highly recommended, winding through tea plantations and mountain mist. Tickets can be booked online in advance through our Planner page or directly at the Colombo Fort station.

How do I apply for an ETA tourist visa?

You can apply online via the official Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) website. It typically processes within 24-48 hours and is valid for a 30-day stay from date of entry.

Can I get a refund if my train is delayed?

Yes, refunds are eligible for cancellations or delays exceeding 2 hours. You can file a claim via our Support dashboard or present your physical ticket at any railway station ticket window.

Are ATMs widely available in the Cultural Triangle?

Yes, commercial towns like Dambulla, Sigiriya, and Anuradhapura have operational ATMs. However, we recommend carrying local cash (LKR) for village shops, heritage entry fees, and local tuk-tuks.
